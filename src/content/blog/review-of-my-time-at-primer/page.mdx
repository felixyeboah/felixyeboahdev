Accepting an offer from [Primer](https://primer.io) was a pivotal decision in my software engineering career. Although my tenure with the company was shorter than I initially planned, the experience opened doors and presented valuable opportunities. I am deeply grateful to everyone involved in the hiring process at Primer, from the first recruiter I spoke with to the final interviewer. Each person saw potential in me and played a crucial role in solidifying their decision.

In December 2021, my girlfriend discovered Primer and took the initiative to apply on my behalf, knowing my desire to explore international job opportunities. I was pleasantly surprised when I received an email from Primer expressing their interest in moving forward with me. That day brought immense joy, yet it paled in comparison to the day I received the official offer letter.

The interview process proceeded smoothly, thanks to the exceptional individuals I encountered during the five stages. Their professionalism and demeanor put me at ease, and their positive feedback reached my recruiter, who proved to be an invaluable ally throughout the process. As they say, a smooth interview journey is often attributed to the support and guidance of a skilled recruiter.

I must give a special shout-out to Kat, my recruiter, who truly went above and beyond during my interview process. She played an instrumental role in making it a seamless and enjoyable experience.

Ultimately, I secured the role of Frontend Engineer and receiving the offer letter marked one of the happiest days of my life. The excitement kept me awake the night before, and even Kat noticed the sparkle in my eyes when she called to share the news of my selection.

During my time at Primer, I had the privilege of directly contributing to the success and growth of the product area. Working closely with cross-functional teams such as Product, Design, and Engineering, I helped bring elegant and intuitive experiences to life. I played a significant role in crucial technology decisions and feature development, focusing on scalability and optimization. Additionally, I reviewed code and continuously aimed to enhance engineering efficiency and best practices. I prioritized optimizing user-facing products and interfaces, documenting projects for both customers and internal teams, and building strong payment expertise to identify areas for improvement and propose proactive solutions.

My experience at Primer was not just professionally fulfilling, but also enriched my personal life. I had the pleasure of meeting exceptional individuals who became good friends. I acquired new skills, enhanced my communication abilities, and honed my coding and critical thinking prowess. Primer fostered a sense of familial connection rather than a mere workplace, and I wholeheartedly recommend it to anyone seeking a remote-first job with a supportive and inclusive environment.

#### In conclusion, here's my review:

- 100% remote-first work culture
- Opportunities for travel and team meetups
- Friendly and welcoming atmosphere among colleagues
- Flexibility to work at your own pace
- Ability to set your own start time
- Strong work-life balance at Primer
- Opportunity for ongoing learning and personal development while working

Although my time at Primer has come to an end, the company will always hold a special place in my heart.
